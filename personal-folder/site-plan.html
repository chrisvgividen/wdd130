Overview

Purpose

This is the start of my own online business. I've always enjoyed buying broken electronics and fixing them, but what do I do with the ones I fix and don't want? Sell them. But I might not have a ton of recognition on Ebay, so this website will help with that. I'll post the items on Ebay, then edit the pictures and pricing to match my websites pictures with the Ebay pictures. Please share this website, it would really help me out!

Audience

This site is for those who want to challenge themselves or others with electronic repairs. Some of the items I sell have problems too advanced for me. Others are too time consuming, and a few (hopefully not too many) will be failed repairs that I can't fix. If you like repairing electronics, this is the place for you!
